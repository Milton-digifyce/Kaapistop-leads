FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any upfront cost to get started?

Absolutely zero upfront cost on our Rental Model. We provide the machine, handle installation, and train your staff — all free. You only pay for the coffee ingredients you consume.

What if the machine breaks down?

Call your dedicated account manager and we guarantee a technician on-site within 4 hours on business days. Emergency support is available 7 days a week.

How often do you refill the machine?

We set a fixed refill schedule based on your consumption (typically weekly). Need an emergency refill? We'll sort it within 24 hours.

What beverages can the machine serve?

South Indian Filter Coffee, Milk Tea, Black Tea, Masala Chai, Hot Milk, and more. We'll customise the menu to your team's preferences during the free demo visit.

Which cities do you serve?

Currently: Coimbatore, Tirupur, Erode, Salem, Chennai, and surrounding Tamil Nadu districts. Fill the form with your city and we'll confirm availability immediately.
